About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Information Systems at the BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus Rajasthan. Prior to this, I was Assistant Professor in the Computer Science department. I was a Postdoctoral scholar at University of Central Florida, USA where I worked alongside Prof. Amrit Singh Bedi. Prior to joining UCF, I worked at One Network Enterprises India Private Limited as ML Scientist. I obtained the Ph.D. and Master of Science dual degree from the Industrial Engineering and Operations Research at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. N. Hemachandra.

I hold a Bachelor of Science (Hons.) degree in Mathematics from Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi.

Internship requests from students outside BITS Pilani are considered only if accompanied by a recommendation from a known academic acquaintance (such as a colleague or collaborator, either within or outside BITS). I may not respond to unsolicited requests for internships.

In addition to my academic work, I am passionate about mentoring the next generation of researchers. I frequently participate in workshops and seminars aimed at bridging the gap between theoretical machine learning and practical, deployable AI systems.
