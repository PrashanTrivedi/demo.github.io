About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at the Indian Institute of Technology, Hyderabad (IIT Hyderabad). Prior to this, I was a Research Fellow in the Institute of Data Science at the National University of Singapore (NUS), where I worked alongside Prof. Vincent Y. F. Tan. Prior to joining NUS, I obtained the Ph.D. and Master of Science (Engineering) dual degree from the Department of Electrical Communication Engineering at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Rajesh Sundaresan.

Much earlier to joining the dual degree programme, I worked as a Project Assistant in Prof. Chandra R. Murthy's lab at IISc, where I was introduced to the world of research. I hold a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electronics and Communications from R V College of Engineering, Bengaluru.

Internship requests from students outside IIT Hyderabad are considered only if accompanied by a recommendation from a known academic acquaintance (such as a colleague or collaborator, either within or outside IIT Hyderabad). I may not respond to unsolicited requests for internships.

In addition to my academic work, I am passionate about mentoring the next generation of researchers. I frequently participate in workshops and seminars aimed at bridging the gap between theoretical machine learning and practical, deployable AI systems.
